Pepperoni pizza

For the dough

This is the dough recipe

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of flour
  • 5 grams of salt
  • 5 grams of dry yeast
  • 200 ml of water
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil

Dough preparation

  1. In a bowl put the flour and in one side the salt and in the other side the yeast and mix it
  2. Now put the water on the bowl, the oil and mix it
  3. Put the mixture in a table and start to knead the dough with your hands about 15 to 20 minute until the dough is smooth
  4. Make a ball with your dough and in the same bowl you used before put a little bit of olive oil and leave the dough rest in there about 2 hours
  5. Passed the 2 hours, put the dough on the table again and stretch it with your hands gentil. Then form a ball again and let it rest insde the bowl again about 2 or 3 hours until it double it size

For the toppings

This are the steps you have to follow when your dough is ready

Toppings

  • 100 grams of tomato sauce
  • 200 grams of mozzarella cheese
  • 200 grams of pepperoni

Pizza preparation

  1. Preheat the oven at maximum temperature
  2. Put the dough on a big plate with a lot of flour and with your finger tips start to press the dough from center to outside formin a disk with thick borders
  3. When your disk is formed put the sauce and spread it on your pizza
  4. Put the mozzarella on top of the sauce (you can grate the cheese) and then spread the pepperoni on top of that
  5. When your oven is preheated put the pizza inside and wait between 10 to 15 minutes (depending your oven temperature) and it will be ready (the borders should be toasted)
