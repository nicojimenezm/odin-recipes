Bacon Cheeseburger

Juicy bacon cheeseburger

This is the recipe of a juicy and simple cheeseburger to make on home!

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • 200 grams of grounded beef
  • Burger bread
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl mix with your hands the grounded beef with salt and pepper
  2. Make a ball of meat with your hands and let it rest
  3. Next, preheat an iron cast or a pan with a litle bit of oil
  4. Then, put the meatball in the heated pan or iron cast and smash it. Meanwhile, in a preheated oven at maximum temperature put slices of bacon for 10 minutes
  5. Cook the burger for 5 minutes and flip it over for another 5 minutes in the other side
  6. Put a slice of cheese on top on the burger and let it rest en the bread for 2 minutes and put the bacon on top
  7. Put mayo, ketchup, mustard or anything you like on the bread and enjoy it!
